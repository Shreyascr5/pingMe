Hi , this is a Firebase based chat application which is made using ReactJS, 
Firebase database link : https://console.firebase.google.com/project/pingyapp5/firestore/data/~2Fmessages

steps to use: 
1. Login using your Google Account.
2.Reload your webpage if your chat application doesn't Load.
3.Enter the Room name as same as your friend's Room name! (Entering a different room name might create a new Chat webpage).
4.Write messages to your friend on the other side and also recieve messages from them.
5. Sign out lets you log out of the room.
